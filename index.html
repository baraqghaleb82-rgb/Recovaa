The Problem

Every AI system today demands your raw data

Regulated sectors — healthcare, fintech, government — cannot afford to expose sensitive personal data to AI systems. But they're being left behind on personalisation as a result.

⚠️

Decryption creates exposure

Current AI architectures require plaintext data during computation, creating insider threat and breach risk at every inference call.

📋

How It Works

Three layers. Zero plaintext.

Recova's architecture mathematically guarantees that sensitive data is never exposed — not to servers, not to administrators, not to anyone.

01

Client-Side Encryption

Data is encrypted at the source using user-held cryptographic keys. Processing organisations never obtain decryption capability, providing IND-CPA security guarantees against insider access.

02

Secure Preprocessing Pipeline

Encrypted data undergoes transformation into computation-ready formats while maintaining full cryptographic protection. No plaintext material exists at rest or in transit.

03

Encrypted Inference Engine

AI recommendation models perform inference natively on encrypted representations using a hybrid framework of functional encryption and garbled circuits — delivering 10–50× performance gains over homomorphic encryption alone.
