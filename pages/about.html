About Recova

Born from world-class research. Built for the real world.

Recova is a CyberASAP-backed innovation project emerging from Edinburgh Napier University, developing the first commercially deployable privacy-preserving AI recommendation engine for regulated sectors.

Our Mission

We believe organisations in healthcare, fintech, and government should never have to choose between delivering intelligent, personalised experiences and protecting the privacy of the people they serve.

Recova eliminates that trade-off entirely. Our encrypted inference engine runs AI recommendation models directly on encrypted data — meaning sensitive personal information is mathematically guaranteed never to be exposed during computation.

This isn't a workaround. It's a fundamental architectural advance: privacy-preserving AI that actually performs at commercial scale.

Our Background

Recova is built on deep expertise in applied cryptography, privacy-preserving computation, and AI systems engineering, developed across years of academic research at Edinburgh Napier University's Centre for Distributed Computing, Networks and Security.

The team brings a proven CyberASAP track record through the successful spin-out of LastingAsset, which attracted over £500,000 in follow-on external funding — demonstrating our ability to navigate the full journey from research to commercial entity.

Recova is supported by Innovate UK through the CyberASAP commercialisation programme (Project No: 10193687), with the goal of establishing a UK-headquartered, globally scalable privacy technology company.
